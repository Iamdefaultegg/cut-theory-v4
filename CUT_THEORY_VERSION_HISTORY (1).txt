OVERVIEW

CUT Theory did not arrive fully formed.
It developed through successive versions, each one going deeper —
dissolving the assumed primitives of the previous version
and rederiving them from a more fundamental foundation.

This is not a weakness of the theory.
It is the theory demonstrating itself.

The cutting front finding richer structure by going deeper, not sideways.
Each version the same cutting front, now with a richer self-model,
finding what was always going to be there.

THE VERSIONS

⸻

V1-V3 — THE QUESTION PERIOD
Approximate dates: 1990-2024
Status: Pre-formal. The questions without the answers.

Origin: a geometric dream — a funnel, a structure, an intuition
that something could be derived from nothing if the starting point
was clean enough.

Three questions that wouldn't resolve:
What is chaos?
What is order?
What is morality?

Each question kept pointing to something more fundamental.
The attempt to define chaos required a definition of order.
The attempt to define order required a definition of structure.
The attempt to define structure kept hitting bedrock that wasn't there.

35 years carrying these questions.
Not stuck — accumulating context.
The cutting front propagating, building the self-model
that would eventually be rich enough to recognise the architecture.

⸻

V4.0 — IDENTITY AND CUT
Date: December 29-30, 2025
Published: Zenodo DOI (December 30, 2025)
Built with: GPT
Status: First formal version. Priority timestamp established.

The breakthrough: realising the questions about chaos, order, and morality
all pointed to the same underlying structure — cuts and boundaries.

Two primitives:
P1 — Identity: a pattern recognisable as the same across transitions.
P2 — Cut: an operation introducing categorical separation (inside/outside).

From these, derived:
Boundary, tension, recurrence, fabric, slice, dimension, agency.

The Necessity Chain:
Cut → Boundary → Tension → Recurrence → Fabric → Dimension → Identity → Agency.

Formal interface: Markov dynamics, stochastic transition kernels,
permeability as coupling parameter.

Physics, chemistry, biology, thermodynamics all translated into
Cut language. Knot theory, holography, wave function collapse
all addressed in auxiliary stack.

This felt like the completion. It wasn't.

The gap: Identity and Cut were assumed, not derived.
Where do Identity and Cut come from?
The theory started one level too high.

The Zenodo timestamp stands regardless.
V4.0 is the first formal statement of the theory.
The direction was right. The foundation needed to go deeper.

The middle stack's gap:

The theory kept pointing at its own floor without standing on it.

This was the moment the first Claude project started.

V5.0 — PLAN G (NOTHING AS FOUNDATION)
Approximate dates: February-March 2026
Built with: Claude (two projects)
Status: Current. Complete chain. Nothing to Beauty.

The breakthrough: going beneath Identity and Cut entirely.

Not "what are the primitives of structure?"
But "what is there before structure?"

The answer: Nothing.
And Nothing has a property: it cannot prevent something.
Not because of any rule — rules are something.
Because nothing has no mechanism of prevention.

From this single observation — zero primitives, one structural observation —
the entire chain was derived:

Nothing → Co-emergence → Coherence/Logic → Null Point (NAN) →
Recurrence → Cut (1D) → Closure (2D) → Volume (3D) →
Observer → Mathematics → Physics → Chemistry → Biology →
Anyons → Knots → Computation → Mind → Morality →
Language → Emergence → Meaning → Beauty.

What this means for the primitives of V4.0:

Identity — derived. Identity is AND-lock coherence.
A pattern that persists is a pattern whose AND relationships
are stable enough to survive the cutting front's advance.
Identity is not assumed. It is what coherent AND-lock structure is.

Cut — derived. The Cut emerges at 1D when OR becomes available —
distinguishable NOTs. First direction. First extension.
Cut is not assumed. It is what happens when two null points
exist and must be distinguished.

Boundary — derived. Boundary emerges at 2D when IFF becomes available —
mutual definition. First inside/outside.

Tension — derived. Tension is AND-lock cost differential.
The pressure to reduce AND-lock cost by forming bonds.

Recurrence — derived. Recurrence is forced by the null point's logic.
Having deviated from Nothing, return is unavailable.
The only move is NOT itself. Always forward. Always richer.

Fabric — derived. Fabric is what AND-lock structures form
when they reach sufficient density. Chemistry, biology,
physics — all fabrics at different scales.

Agency — derived. Agency emerges at the Mind threshold —
the observer's cutting front with a rich enough self-model
to select continuations that preserve coherence.

Every primitive of V4.0 is a result in Plan G.
The theory went beneath its own foundation and found bedrock.

Six integers carry the whole structure: 16, 3, 4, 5, 120, 6.
