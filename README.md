# Cut Theory — Plan G (V5.0)

A unified theory of everything derived from a single logical operation:

*IF nothing has zero positive properties, THEN it cannot prevent
anything. Therefore everything exists — and we can stand here
and ponder the paradox of nothing.*

## What this is

Cut Theory derives the structure of physical reality, life, mind,
morality, and meaning from that single claim. No external assumptions.
No free parameters beyond six integers.

The chain runs from Nothing to Beauty. Unbroken.

## Current version — V5.0 (Plan G)

V5.0 derives everything from Nothing. The primitives of V4.0
(Identity and Cut) are results here, not assumptions.

So far, it seems, that we have identified six integers. These may
not be the final and complete set. Or they might be: 16, 3, 4, 5, 120, 6.

The structure builds a coherent dimensional stack from both ends
simultaneously — logic and geometry from the ground up, and a GOD
frame from above where 3D breaks down and we interpret what remains.

The primary open problem is the formal bridge that upgrades n=16
from MOTIVATED to DERIVED. If that falls, much of the rest may
follow. But the theory is young and the full problem set is still
emerging.

## Epistemic standards

All results are flagged throughout:
- DERIVED — forced by logical necessity. Could not be otherwise.
- MOTIVATED — structurally required, not yet formally closed.
- SPECULATIVE — open conjecture. Requires formal proof or test.
